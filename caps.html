Welcome to Counseling and Psychological Services

University Health & Counseling (UHC) supports the health and well-being of the campus community. We are inspired to help students realize their potential, cope with the stresses of life, work productively, and connect meaningfully with others. UHC is made up of Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS), which offers counseling services, and University Health Service (UHS), which offers medical services.

Whether you're feeling overwhelmed, stressed, or just need someone to talk to, remember you're not alone. Taking small steps to care for your mind can make a big difference.
